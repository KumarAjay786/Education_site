Educational Loan Assistance

Clarity on students’ future

How PRAGATHI can assist you to avail educational loan?

Don’t let your dreams fade off just because you don’t have money to fulfill them. Both Pragathi and Commercial bank nearby you to encourage students to complete their educational dreams by providing them easy loans at low rates.

As per RBI instruction, all the commercial liable to give loan of Rs.4 Lakh in personal guarantee. Rate of interest for this loan is PLR+1% only & repayment is starting 1 year after the course completing.

We are having a legal team, who are experts in this area, will support the student to avail educational loan from the bank.

Eligibility:

As per decision by RBI instruction, min 60% marks in eligible course is the eligibility to apply for educational loan.

If the applicant’s father or mother has undergone Civil hitting (scoring less than 600), until their son /daughter reach 18 years old, they are not eligible for educational loan.

How Pragathi helps students to get educational Loan:

We prepare all documents required for loan. For applying Online, login to www.vidhyalakshmi.com and students can apply directly/ or from the college office we do this.
