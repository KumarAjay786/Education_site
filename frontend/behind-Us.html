BEHIND US

Pragathi group of Institutions is functioning under the
Sree. Kanteerava Pragathi Vidyasamste holding Reg. No S No. 254/85-86,
the society registered in the year 1986, under Karnataka Societies registration Ac No. 17 of 1960.

Objective of Society

  • Provide international quality education to student
  • Conduct skill development program to student
  • By providing skill training make them self-dependent
  • Empower girls by providing quality education with low cost
  • Promotion of Sports and cultural activities in the campus
  • Support financially back word students by providing scholarship & sponsorship
  • Up lift the down trodden people by starting handicraft center in order to get the benefit
  • Make the handicapped people self-dependent through providing training them to production field by using their working missiles
