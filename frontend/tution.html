Class Information

At Pragathi Group of Institutions, we are committed to shaping the future of students by providing high-quality tuition services for classes 9 to 12. With years of experience and a team of highly qualified educators, we offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to help students excel in their academic journey. Our personalized teaching approach focuses on understanding each student's unique needs, ensuring they achieve their full potential in subjects like Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer Science.

Whether you're looking to improve your grades, prepare for board exams, or build a strong foundation for higher education, Pragathi Group of Institutions is here to guide you every step of the way.

Why Choose Us ?

  • Experienced Faculty : Our teachers are experts in their respective subjects with years of teaching experience.
  • Personalized Attention : We provide one-on-one sessions and small group tutoring to ensure each student's needs are met.
  • Proven Success Rate : Our students consistently perform well in their board exams and entrance tests.
  • Interactive Learning : We combine traditional teaching methods with modern educational tools to engage students and encourage active learning.
  • Regular Assessments : Our regular tests and feedback help track student progress and improve weak areas.
